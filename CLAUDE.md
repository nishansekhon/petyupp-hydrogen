# PetYupp Hydrogen

## Brand Vision
"Journey of life — share it with your furry buddy."

PetYupp is a lifestyle brand, not just pet e-commerce. Three pillars:
1. AI-powered problem-first discovery — conversational, not catalog browsing
2. Lifestyle elevation — a dog makes YOUR life richer, healthier, more joyful
3. Natural earth-made philosophy — dogs deserve nature, not factories

Design language: warm (cream #FDF8F4, not white), sunlit photography, real human+dog moments, natural textures. 80% professional / 20% trendy. All buttons teal. Every UX decision should evoke warmth, connection, and shared journey.

## Tech Stack
- Shopify Hydrogen (React) on Oxygen
- Storefront API for products
- Anthropic Claude API for AI advisor
- Cloudinary for images (cloud: petyupp-lifestyle)
- Dev: npx shopify hydrogen dev
- Deploy: auto-deploy from GitHub main branch
